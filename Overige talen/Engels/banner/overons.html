ABOUT US

TRAINING

At Alphabet Soup Translations, we are passionate about language and dedicated to conveying your message clearly and effectively across different languages. With a background in Applied Linguistics from Ghent University, we specialise in text revision, proofreading, and translation in Dutch, French, English, and Spanish.

TEAM MEMBERS

Our team consists of three enthusiastic twenty-somethings: Laura, Tine, and Elise. Tine is a native Dutch speaker and translates to and from Dutch, English, and Spanish. In addition, she revises and proofreads texts and handles the administrative side of the company. Elise, also a native Dutch speaker, translates to and from Dutch, French, and Spanish. She carries out revisions and proofreading alongside her financial responsibilities within the company. Besides acquiring clients, revising, and proofreading texts, Laura also focuses on translations. As a native Dutch speaker, she effortlessly translates to and from Dutch, English, and Spanish.
